HOW TO STOP

Human trafficking is the fastest growing crime in the U.S. Our teenagers
and young adults are the primary target with recruitment taking place in schools,
malls, and social media. Human trafficking is often a crime of psychology, luring
in unsuspecting victims with promises of love and easy money.
That means EDUCATION is the key to keep kids safe, parents informed and our schools
and community engaged in stopping the exploitation of our young people.


  • Educational App

Project Description

University of California, Irvine recognizes that human trafficking is a global issue
and that a solution is needed to educate people all over the world on human trafficking
and how it affects lives. Team DJANIC is partnering with Million Kids, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit
organization, to develop a smartphone application. This application’s main functionality is
to educate the world on the nature of human trafficking and how to react in situations in
which the user feels they, or someone they love, may be a victim of human trafficking.
